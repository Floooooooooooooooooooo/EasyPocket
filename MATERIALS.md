# EasyPocket - Materials & Sourcing Guide

## Required Materials for Prototype V1

### Primary Materials

#### 1. TPU Filament (for 3D Printing Method)
- **Quantity:** 50g per unit (order 500g spool)
- **Type:** Flexible TPU filament
- **Specifications:**
  - Shore Hardness: A 60-70
  - Diameter: 1.75mm or 2.85mm (match your printer)
  - Color: Black (prototype)
- **Recommended Brands:**
  - NinjaFlex (Premium)
  - SainSmart TPU (Budget)
  - PolyFlex (Mid-range)
- **Cost:** $20-35 per 500g spool
- **Suppliers:**
  - Amazon
  - MatterHackers
  - 3D printing specialty stores

#### 2. Adhesive Backing
- **Type:** 3M VHB Tape or equivalent
- **Specifications:**
  - Thickness: 0.5mm
  - Size: 50mm x 30mm per unit
  - Removable formula
  - Surface compatible: Plastic, silicone, metal
- **Product Options:**
  - 3M VHB Tape 5952
  - Gorilla Heavy Duty Mounting Tape
  - Generic nano adhesive tape
- **Cost:** $8-15 per roll (enough for 50+ units)
- **Suppliers:**
  - Hardware stores
  - Amazon
  - Office supply stores

### Alternative Materials (Hand-Built Prototype)

#### 3. Fabric Option
- **Material:** Neoprene or canvas
- **Thickness:** 2-3mm
- **Size:** 200mm x 150mm per unit
- **Cost:** $5-10 per yard (makes 10+ units)
- **Suppliers:** Fabric stores

#### 4. Leather Option
- **Material:** Genuine or faux leather
- **Thickness:** 1-2mm
- **Size:** 200mm x 150mm per unit
- **Cost:** $10-20 per sq ft
- **Suppliers:** Craft stores, Tandy Leather

#### 5. Craft Foam Option (Testing Only)
- **Material:** EVA foam sheets
- **Thickness:** 3mm
- **Size:** 300mm x 200mm sheets
- **Cost:** $3-5 per sheet (makes 5+ units)
- **Suppliers:** Craft stores, Amazon

### Supporting Materials

#### 6. Thread/Adhesive (for Hand-Built)
- **Thread:** Heavy-duty polyester or nylon
- **Size:** Tex 70-90
- **Color:** Match material color
- **Cost:** $3-5 per spool
- **Adhesive:** E6000 or contact cement
- **Cost:** $5-8 per tube

#### 7. Elastic Bands (Alternative Attachment)
- **Type:** Flat elastic band
- **Width:** 20mm
- **Length:** 150mm per unit
- **Cost:** $3-5 per meter
- **Purpose:** Alternative to adhesive for testing

#### 8. RFID Blocking Material (Optional Enhancement)
- **Type:** RFID blocking fabric or aluminum sheet
- **Thickness:** 0.1mm
- **Size:** 90mm x 60mm per unit
- **Cost:** $10-15 per meter (makes 50+ units)
- **Suppliers:** Amazon, specialty electronics stores

### Tools Required

#### For 3D Printing
- [ ] 3D Printer (FDM with flexible filament capability)
- [ ] Flush cutters or snips
- [ ] Sandpaper (fine grit, 220-400)
- [ ] Needle nose pliers (for support removal)
- [ ] Craft knife
- [ ] Ruler/calipers

#### For Hand-Built
- [ ] Sharp scissors or rotary cutter
- [ ] Cutting mat
- [ ] Ruler and marking tool
- [ ] Sewing machine (optional) or needle
- [ ] Clamps or clips
- [ ] Iron (for certain materials)

#### Measurement & Testing
- [ ] Digital calipers (0.01mm precision)
- [ ] Credit cards (for fit testing)
- [ ] Test phone or phone mock-up
- [ ] Scale (for weight measurement)
- [ ] Camera (for documentation)

## Sourcing Options

### Option 1: Local 3D Printing Service
- **Use Case:** No printer available
- **Cost:** $15-30 per print
- **Timeline:** 2-5 days
- **Find:** Makerspaces, UPS Store, local 3D print shops

### Option 2: Online 3D Printing Service
- **Services:**
  - Shapeways
  - Sculpteo
  - Xometry
  - 3D Hubs
- **Cost:** $20-40 per print + shipping
- **Timeline:** 1-2 weeks
- **Advantage:** Professional finish, material choices

### Option 3: DIY with Printer
- **Cost:** $30-50 initial materials
- **Timeline:** Same day
- **Advantage:** Rapid iteration
- **Requirement:** Access to FDM 3D printer

### Option 4: Hand-Craft Prototype
- **Cost:** $10-20 per unit
- **Timeline:** 2-3 hours
- **Advantage:** No special equipment
- **Best for:** Initial concept validation

## Material Safety

### TPU Filament
- **Safety:** Low toxicity
- **Ventilation:** Recommended during printing
- **Handling:** Wear gloves if sensitive skin
- **Storage:** Dry, sealed container

### Adhesives
- **Safety:** Follow manufacturer guidelines
- **Ventilation:** Required for some types
- **Handling:** Avoid skin contact
- **Storage:** Cool, dry place

### General Safety
- Always work in well-ventilated area
- Use appropriate protective equipment
- Follow tool safety guidelines
- Dispose of materials properly

## Lead Times
- TPU Filament: 2-5 days (online), same day (local)
- Adhesive: 2-5 days (online), same day (local)
- Hand-craft materials: Same day (local craft store)
- 3D printing service: 3-14 days
- International shipping: 2-4 weeks

## Quality Considerations
- **TPU:** Avoid old or moisture-damaged filament
- **Adhesive:** Check expiration dates
- **Fabric/Leather:** Inspect for defects before cutting
- **All materials:** Order samples if bulk buying

## Storage Recommendations
- **TPU Filament:** Airtight container with desiccant
- **Adhesive:** Original packaging, room temperature
- **Fabric/Materials:** Flat, dry storage
- **Tools:** Organized toolbox or drawer

## Sustainability Notes
- TPU is recyclable (check local facilities)
- Consider bio-based TPU options
- Fabric scraps can be saved for testing
- Minimize waste through careful planning
- Adhesive backing can be reused for testing
